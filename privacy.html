Ampereway Electric Inc — Privacy Policy

Information We Collect

We collect customer information such as name, phone number, email address, service address, and details related to requested electrical services.

How We Use This Information

  • Schedule and confirm appointments
  • Send service updates
  • Provide estimates and invoices
  • Communicate regarding electrical services
  • Improve customer support

Information Sharing

We do not sell or share customer information with third parties except service providers necessary to complete electrical work or to comply with legal requirements.

SMS Consent

When customers provide their phone number and request electrical service, they consent to receive SMS messages related to scheduling, service updates, and billing. SMS consent is not shared with third parties or affiliates.

Opt-Out Instructions

Customers may reply “STOP” at any time to opt out of SMS messages.
